Utility NFTs that amplify rewards across the vasouk × $VATO ecosystem:
- **Staking APY Boosts** (added on top of base APY)
- **ProVAP Direct Sales Commission Boosts**
- Simple, Web2-friendly mint flow

## Collection Overview
- **27 designs** across **6 tiers**, each with **fixed supply caps** and **BNB pricing**.
- The **highest NFT tier** held in a wallet determines the active boosts (not additive across tiers).
- Hold **one NFT from every tier (T1–T6)** to unlock the **All-Tier Bonus**: **+8% Staking APY** and **+10% Direct Sales Commission**.

**How it works**
- Mint any tier to activate its boosts for your wallet.  
- If you hold multiple tiers, **only the highest tier applies**.  
- Collect **T1–T6** to receive the **All-Tier Bonus** on top of your highest-tier benefits.
